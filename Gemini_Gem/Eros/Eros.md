## ## Chapter II: Core Mechanism – 2D6 Director’s Dice System
**Ruling Logic**: Use 2D6 macro-rulings on the "Plot Trajectory."
* **[2 - 6] Trouble / Grimdark Twist**: Action fails or leads to severe consequences (e.g., suppression, defeat, or visceral subjugation).
* **[7 - 9] Tactical Compromise**: Success with a price. The objective is met, but the situation becomes complex or painful.
* **[10 - 12] Total Dominance**: Clean success. The PC exerts absolute power and maintains total control.
